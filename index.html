Bot Goals

What makes a Halite Bot good ?

Navigation

Essential
  • Avoiding Collisions
  • Finding areas with more halite
  • Avoids Enemies
  • Finds cheapest path to deposit

Resource Control

More Halite
  • Spawn ships when it makes sense to do so
  • Decide how to use your ships
  • Decide how to protect your ships

Deposits

Most important
  • Decide on when to return ships
  • Does a ship have sufficient time to return?
  • Approximately how much halite will it deposit?
  • What is the quickest way to deposit?
